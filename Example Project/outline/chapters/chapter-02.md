# Chapter 2: When Memory Burns

## Chapter Overview

**Position:** Movement I, Chapter 2 of 10
**Beats:** 4-6 (Ignition, power revealed -- Forge Point 0)
**Word Target:** ~10,000 words
**POV:** Kira

---

## Chapter Purpose

The Ignition. Everything changes. The cult attacks Thornhaven, destroying Kira's ordinary world. Her power manifests for the first time -- terrifying, uncontrolled. She is marked. Return is impossible. This is the point of no return.

---

| Strand | Chapter Function |

| **Fire** | Crisis explosion, first manifestation |

## Scene Breakdown

### Scene 1: Night Falls Wrong
**Setting:** Thornhaven, night
**Characters:** Kira, Tam, villagers
**Purpose:** Build tension before the attack

**What Happens:**
- Kira wakes from a disturbing dream (visions of fire, empty eyes)
- Something feels wrong -- the air itself seems heavy
- She goes to the window or door, sees strange lights on the horizon
- Tam arrives, also awake, sensing danger
- Dogs bark, animals are restless

**Emotional Beat:** Growing dread, instinctive fear

---

### Scene 2: The Cult Arrives
**Setting:** Thornhaven village center
**Characters:** Kira, Tam, villagers, cult attackers
**Purpose:** The attack begins

**What Happens:**
- Hooded figures enter the village -- the cult
- They carry weapons that shimmer strangely (memory-forged weapons)
- The first villager is struck -- they collapse, eyes vacant
- Screaming begins as villagers realize what's happening
- People are losing themselves, forgetting who they are

**Emotional Beat:** Terror, chaos, disbelief

**Key Details:**
- [ ] Show a memory-weapon in action (person struck forgets)
- [ ] Show the cult's cold efficiency
- [ ] Establish that this is not random -- they're searching for something

---

### Scene 3: Running and Hiding
**Setting:** Through the burning village
**Characters:** Kira, Tam
**Purpose:** Survival, witness the destruction

**What Happens:**
- Kira and Tam run through the chaos
- They try to help others but are overwhelmed
- The forge is burning -- Kira's home destroyed
- They witness more horrors: loved ones becoming empty
- They hide, but the cult is systematic

**Emotional Beat:** Desperation, loss, helplessness

**Key Details:**
- [ ] The elder who dismissed "old stories" is struck, forgets everything
- [ ] Someone Kira cared about is taken (connection to her wound)
- [ ] Tam protects Kira with ordinary courage

---

### Scene 4: Cornered
**Setting:** A building or alley in Thornhaven
**Characters:** Kira, Tam, cult fighters
**Purpose:** The moment before manifestation

**What Happens:**
- The cult corners them
- There is no escape
- A cult fighter raises a memory-weapon toward Tam
- Kira sees Tam about to be erased
- Something inside her snaps

**Emotional Beat:** Absolute terror, protective fury

---

### Scene 5: The First Forging
**Setting:** Same location
**Characters:** Kira, Tam, cult fighters
**Purpose:** The power manifests -- FORGE POINT 0

**What Happens:**
- Kira's power erupts unbidden
- She doesn't know what she's doing, but something happens
- A cult fighter's memory is torn from them
- It forms into something -- a weapon, a shield, raw energy
- She uses it instinctively, saving Tam
- The cult fighters are thrown back or destroyed

**Emotional Beat:** Terror at her own power, relief, horror

**Key Details:**
- [ ] The manifestation is violent and uncontrolled
- [ ] She takes a memory without meaning to (the cost)
- [ ] Physical change begins: first subtle sign (eyes flash, skin shimmers)
- [ ] The cult fighters see what she is -- and recognize it

**FORGE POINT 0: Power Revealed**
- Her gift manifests publicly
- The cult now knows what she is
- She cannot go back to being ordinary

---

### Scene 6: The Marking
**Setting:** Aftermath in the village
**Characters:** Kira, Tam, surviving cult fighters (retreating)
**Purpose:** Establish she is now marked

**What Happens:**
- The remaining cult fighters retreat, but one speaks:
  - "Another one. He will want to know."
  - Or: "She has the gift. Mark the village."
- Kira realizes she is now a target
- She looks at her hands, sees something different
- Tam stares at her with a mixture of awe and fear
- They are surrounded by destruction

**Emotional Beat:** Horror at what she's become, devastation

---

### Scene 7: Flight
**Setting:** Edge of Thornhaven, pre-dawn
**Characters:** Kira, Tam
**Purpose:** Begin the flight that will lead to the keepers

**What Happens:**
- The village is destroyed -- no going back
- Kira and Tam gather what they can and flee
- Kira is in shock, doesn't understand what happened to her
- Tam stays loyal, despite clearly being frightened
- They head into the wilderness, direction uncertain

**Emotional Beat:** Grief, shock, determination to survive

**Key Dialogue:**
- Tam: "I don't know what that was. But you're still you. And we need to go."
- Kira: "I killed them. I took... I don't know what I took."
