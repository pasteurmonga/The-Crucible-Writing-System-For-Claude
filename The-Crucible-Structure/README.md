# The Crucible Structure Documentation

Welcome to the complete documentation for **The Crucible Structure**, a 36-beat narrative framework designed for epic fantasy storytelling.

---

## What Is This?

The Crucible Structure is a comprehensive story architecture that weaves together three narrative strands across five movements, culminating in a powerful climax and resolution. Whether you are using the Crucible plugin in Obsidian or crafting your story manually, this documentation will guide you through every element of the framework.

---

## Who Is This For?

- **Writers** looking for a robust structure to shape their epic fantasy novels
- **Obsidian plugin users** who want to understand the beats and mechanics behind the tool
- **Story architects** interested in deep character transformation and thematic resonance
- **Anyone** who wants to write stories with emotional depth and narrative momentum

## Key Concepts at a Glance

- **Three Strands**: Quest (external journey), Fire (internal transformation), Constellation (relationships)
- **Five Movements + Coda**: The macro-rhythm of your story from opening to resolution
- **Four Forge Points + Apex**: Major turning points where everything changes
- **Mercy Engine**: Four mercies that reveal character through choices
- **Dark Mirror**: Your antagonist as a thematic reflection of your protagonist
