# Mercy Ledger Worksheet

Track the four acts of mercy your protagonist extends and how they pay off.

---

## Understanding the Mercy Ledger

Each mercy should:
- Cost the protagonist something immediate
- Seem like a poor tactical choice at the time
- Pay off unexpectedly in Beat 31 (The Repayment)
- Demonstrate character growth
