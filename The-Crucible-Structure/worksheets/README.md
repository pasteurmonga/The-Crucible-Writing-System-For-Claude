## Completed Examples

See the [examples/](examples/) folder for completed worksheets based on "The Memory Forge" -- a sample story that demonstrates how to fill out each planning document.

---

## Recommended Order

1. **Story Foundation** -- Establish your premise and theme first
2. **Three Strands** -- Map your Quest, Fire, and Constellation
3. **Dark Mirror** -- Design your antagonist as a thematic opposite
4. **Character Constellation** -- Profile your key characters
5. **Forge Points** -- Plan all five crisis points
6. **Mercy Ledger** -- Track your mercy engine
7. **Beat Planning** -- Map beats to chapters

---

## Tips for Use

- **Print them out** -- Writing by hand helps crystallize ideas
- **Reference the examples** -- See how each field should be filled
- **Cross-reference** -- Elements should connect across worksheets
- **Iterate** -- Plans evolve; update worksheets as you discover more
