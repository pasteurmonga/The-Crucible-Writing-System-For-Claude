## One-Sentence Premise

Write your story in one compelling sentence (25 words or less):

A young blacksmith discovers she can forge weapons that steal memories, and must choose whether to become the weapon or its guardian.

---

## Core Forging Question

Complete the following template:

"Will **Kira, a village blacksmith with awakening power**

**learn to master her memory-forging gift without losing her humanity**

before **the cult leader destroys the last memory-keepers and erases the old ways forever**?"

---

## Theme Statement

What truth about human nature does your story explore? (One sentence)

Identity is choice, not origin -- what we do with power matters more than having it.

## Notes

- The memory-forging power has a physical transformation cost
- Cult leader shares same power but made different choices
- Mentor figure will die demonstrating surrender as strength
- Four acts of mercy pay off at the climax
- Childhood friend provides emotional anchor throughout
